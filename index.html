How the VitaTalent workflow works

We plug into your current stack (or your funnels at hire/apply) and take over the messy part of hiring: sourcing, screening, and shortlisting.

1. Role intake & calibration

We align on the role, your non-negotiables, examples of great versus bad hires, and what “success in 90 days” looks like.

2. Sourcing & async screening

Candidates flow in through your funnels or our network. We run structured screening so you don’t have to sit through dozens of calls.

3. Shortlist & decision support

You receive a concise shortlist with notes, recordings (if applicable), and clear next steps. You pick who to interview and move to offer.
